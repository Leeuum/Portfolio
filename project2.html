Role: 3D Generalist (Pre-vis, Layout, Modeling & Rendering)
Studio: Akoda Studio

At Akoda Studio, I worked with a talented team of artists to produce a series of educational animations focused on climate change, green energy, and emerging technologies. My main role was in pre-visualization and camera animation, helping shape scenes in ways that made complex ideas visually clear and compelling.

A lot of my time went into layout, deciding how shots were framed and how scenes flowed to support the narrative. Beyond that, I took on a range of generalist tasks, from modeling and shading to lighting and rendering, always aiming for a balance between visual appeal and technical efficiency.

It was a creatively demanding project, but also one of the most fulfilling. The team at Akoda Studio was sharp, supportive, and genuinely collaborative. You can see their work at akodastudio.com.
