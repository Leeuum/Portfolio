About AKODA

Project: Akoda Studio – Educational Animation Series
Role: 3D Generalist (Pre-vis, Layout, Modeling & Rendering)
Studio: Akoda Studio

At Akoda Studio I worked with a talented team of artists on a series of educational animations covering climate change, green energy, and emerging technologies. My focus was pre-visualisation and camera animation, turning complex ideas into visuals that read clearly and land well.

Most of my time went into layout: how shots were framed, how scenes flowed to support the narrative. Alongside that I took on broader generalist work, from modeling and shading to lighting and rendering, always balancing visual quality against technical efficiency.

Creatively demanding, and one of the most fulfilling projects I've worked on. The team was sharp, supportive, and genuinely collaborative. More of their work at akodastudio.com.
