About MotorScrubber

Project: Product Animation & Advertising Renders
Role: 3D Generalist
Company: MotorScrubber

At MotorScrubber, I worked as the sole 3D artist, creating high-quality visuals of their cleaning products for use across print, digital, and promotional campaigns. I collaborated closely with the in-house graphic designer to make sure everything aligned with the brand's visual identity and marketing strategy.

The standout project was a full product showcase animation for their floor-cleaning tool, BLADE. It was my first major job outside university and a full-pipeline challenge including storyboarding, animatics, pre-vis, animation, lighting, and rendering. I handled every stage myself. It was demanding, fast-paced, and a huge step forward in terms of experience.

That project taught me how to carry a piece from concept through to delivery. It was a pivotal moment early in my career. You can find more about the company at motorscrubberclean.com.
