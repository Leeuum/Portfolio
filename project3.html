About MotorScrubber

Project: Product Animation & Advertising Renders
Role: 3D Generalist
Company: MotorScrubber

I was the sole 3D artist at MotorScrubber, creating high-quality visuals of their cleaning products for print, digital, and promotional campaigns. I worked closely with the in-house graphic designer to keep everything aligned with the brand's visual identity and marketing strategy.

The standout project was a full product showcase animation for BLADE, their floor-cleaning tool. My first major job out of university, and a full-pipeline build covering storyboarding, animatics, pre-vis, animation, lighting, and rendering, with every stage handled myself. Demanding, fast-paced, and a huge step forward.

It taught me how to carry a piece from concept all the way through to delivery, and it remains a pivotal moment early in my career. More about the company at motorscrubberclean.com.
